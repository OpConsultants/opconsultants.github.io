Xactimate Estimates

Our Xactimate estimate includes everything needed to do the job right. An estimate can be written in Xactimate as soon as the full scope of loss is assessed. It can be done to initiate a new claim or to respond to an adjuster’s estimate that you feel is too low or missing necessary items. Going into an adjuster meeting with an Xactimate estimate can help expedite the inspection process and future dealings with an adjuster. Call us if you are just too busy to handle complicated estimates. If you need an Xactimate estimate to settle a claim, OP Consultants are here to help. What is Xactimate? It’s a software which allows contractors and adjusters to draft detailed projects and their damages, as well as to generate estimates of loss so claims can be accurately measured, agreed upon, and paid. Xactimate is used by most insurance companies to price materials and labor. Xactimate provides zip code specific pricing in the US and Canada, and is updated monthly. An Xactimate estimate outlines the specific items necessary to restore the property. Xactimate is the software that we use for preparing your estimates. It allows us to accurately determine the cost of repairs. The settlement of a claim is often a process of negotiation as to what is fair and reasonable in achieving the goal of restoring the contractual rights of the insured. A well written Xactimate estimate bolsters the negotiating posture of the party explaining its opinion of the right corrective course.
